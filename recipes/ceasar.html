Ceasar Salad Recipes

Description

Caesar salad is a crisp, refreshing salad made with romaine lettuce, Parmesan cheese, and crunchy croutons, all tossed in a creamy garlic-lemon dressing. It's a classic dish known for its bold, tangy flavor and satisfying texture. Often served as a starter or light main course, it can also be topped with grilled chicken for extra protein.


Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 2 anchovy fillets (optional, for authentic flavor)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 egg yolk (or 1 tablespoon mayonnaise as a substitute)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

How to prepare it?

  1. Make the dressing: In a bowl, whisk together minced garlic, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and egg yolk (or mayo). Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking until emulsified. Add anchovies if using, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Prepare the lettuce: Tear or chop romaine into bite-sized pieces and place in a large salad bowl.
  3. Add toppings: Sprinkle grated Parmesan and croutons over the lettuce.
  4. Toss and serve: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat. Serve immediately.
